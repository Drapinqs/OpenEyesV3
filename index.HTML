About Us / FAQ

What exactly is OpenEyes?

OpenEyes is a first of its kind! We're a nonprofit orginization aiming to make a familiar face with the tech community. We wish to provide low income families and individuals with technology they can afford.

What does OpenEyes do?

We host multiple events for meetups and gatherings all around. We keep our donations always open for anyone to help us provide! You can attend our gala(s) here.

Can OpenEyes support my family?

If you are interested in seeing if you qualify for our platform, please feel free to contact us through our Contact Page so that we can connect with you.
