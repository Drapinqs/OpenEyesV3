OSINTFramework, OSINTTechniques
In the digital age, online activity leaves behind a trail of information known as "Digital Breadcrumbs." This term refers to the data that individuals leave behind while using the internet. Typically, Digital Breadcrumbs are used to conduct background checks and assess the suitability of job candidates. However, it is also possible to use Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) to locate your own Digital Breadcrumbs on the web.
